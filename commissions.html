Terms and Conditions

I hold the right to turn down any commission that makes me uncomfortable or I feel that I won't be able to draw properly. My signature will be present in all my work. If there is something that you want me to draw that is not mentioned in the list, feel free to reach out!


FAQ

Will the work be printed?

No, I will not be printing any of the work, but you will be receiving a high definition through email in CMYK which makes it optimized for printing :)

How long would it take?

Around 1-2 weeks. This includes me sending you drafts and then the final project once you are happy!

How do I pay you?

You can e-transfer or PayPal me. I take 1/3rd of the cost upfront and then you can transfer the rest once the work is done.
